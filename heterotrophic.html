Algorithm · per layer l
! BELOW water table → saturated (anaerobic) ! ABOVE water table → unsaturated (aerobic) WSG = WL·θ / POR(l) ! saturation f_T = Q10(T_l)^(0.1·(T_l−15)) ! temp factor R_aer = k(l)·C(l)·f_T·(1−WSG) ! aerobic R_anox = k(l)·C(l)·f_T·WSG·F_ANOX ! anaerobic R(l) = 2.64·(R_aer + R_anox) ! µmol/m²/s R_het = Σ R(l) over 23 layers
